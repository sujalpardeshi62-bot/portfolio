Welcome to VR Salon

Welcome to VR Salon, a professional salon in Kalyan East offering modern haircuts, grooming, hair treatments, hair colour, hair spa, and nail services for men and women. Our goal is to give every client a stylish, confident, and premium salon experience.

VR Salon is owned by Ganesh Tiwari, who believes in quality service, clean work, modern styling, and customer satisfaction. Whether you need a fresh haircut, beard styling, hair treatment, colour service, or nail care, VR Salon is here to give you the best look with professional care.
